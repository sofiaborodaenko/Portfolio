My Projects

These are some of the projects that I have created

Red Sunset

Red Sunset is an educational game developed in collaboration with two University of Toronto professors, designed as a modification of Social Democracy: An Alternate History for use in a 400-level history course. In this interactive experience, players step into the role of Mikhail Gorbachev, making critical decisions on policy, resource management, and real-world historical events. I developed the content template that students used to contribute scenarios and data, and was responsible for integrating their work into the game—ensuring a seamless, engaging experience that bridges gameplay with historical education.

UniConnect

UniConnect is a centralized platform that aggregates and displays events from various University of Toronto colleges. In collaboration with three other people, UniConnect was built with a custom tree data structure for efficient filtering by date, college, and category, the app uses a Flask frontend and a backend powered by web scraping and LLM-assisted data extraction. UniConnect streamlines event discovery for students and promotes greater campus engagement.
